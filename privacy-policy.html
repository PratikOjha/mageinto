Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our contact form, request a quote, or otherwise communicate with us. This may include:

  • Personal identifiers: your full name and email address.
  • Contact details: phone number and physical address, if provided.
  • Project details: subject and message content submitted via our contact form.
  • Usage data: browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on the site, collected automatically via standard server logs.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health data through our website.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies help us understand traffic patterns and improve site functionality.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of our site may not function properly without cookies.

We do not use cookies to track you across third-party websites or serve targeted advertisements.
